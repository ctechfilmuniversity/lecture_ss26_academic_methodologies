*Submission*: Broad context Realtime Ai slowly integrates in the field of Live audiovisual performances. Programs for realtime Vjing like Touchdesigner get more ecsessable integrations of real time diffusions through APIs like Stream Diffusion, which proved an possibility to change visuals in realtim through prompts.

What is the problem? Even tho the underlying model of streamDiffusion is StableDiffusion, a widly used model trained on a huge amount of data, the endless possibilty it provides is not gives Artists not always the degree of freedom they want to implement in their work.

Why is the problem important? In other Ai environments, artists are given the posibility train an adittional LoRa on their own art, which gives them more controll over the otutput interpretated by the artificial Intelligence and enables to integrate their artistic freedom in their Work, which shifts the Ais contribution more towords an sparring partner than it doing most of the visual work.

What has so far been done on the problem? Controlls in Touchdesigner include things like the choise of input signal like video and audio and different Tops and so on. In Streamdiffusion, the clearly available changable parameters are promts, seeds, guidance Scale, general model selection, Image input, video input, ControlNet Parameters like Depth maps, audio input and FX Processors

What is the contribution of the paper on the problem? Testing the general control mechanisms currently offered through Touchdesigner and Streamdiffusion, Thesting the time, skills and resources it takes to train and implement a custom trained loRA, if possile at all. Testing the controll mechanisms on consumer Hardware to verify if it's usable for real time AVs
