### Task 03.02 - Storylines

Answer the following questions (which are based on the [Tripod method](../../02_scripts/am_03_questions_script.md#the-tripod)):

I. Complete the following three sentences:

1. *I am examining/working on/writing about ...*   → What am I writing about? (name your research topic)
2. *... because I want to find out/understand/show ...*  → What do I want to know? (name your research question)
3. *... in order to understand/determine/verify...*   → Why do I want to gain this knowledge? (state your research goal)


II. Reformulate what you have written:  

1. Rephrase 3. by finishing the following sentence: *The goal of this paper is to ...*
2. Reformulate 2. as a question.

III. Define your methodology:  
1. Describe in one sentence how you are planning to answer the question formulated in II.2. by finishing the sentence: *I answer the research question...*

  
  
*Submission:* Submit I - III for up to three different ideas. If you are already decided, you can submit fewer, but you must decide on max. three directions. This submission are not the final questions yet.
