### Task 05.01 - Final Paper Description


In my research paper I ask how design features of interactive installations in curated exhibition spaces affect the engagement threshold of first-time visitors. For that I will be reading exisiting literature, analysing interactive installations in terms of the visitor engagement. To define, what can be counted as engagement, I choose to look at quantitave engagement observation since it can be measured and analyzed precisely but with the analysis of qualitative follow-up. This could include: Dwell time, time until first interaction, number of interactions and inputs, "depth" of interaction. Which categories exactly will depend on the installations I find in existing research papers.
In the paper [3] a framework is developed describing the behavioral phases from passing by to active interaction, through quantitative data. [1] gives a simple quantitative approach on measuring engagement in an interactive installation where visitors controll music with their movemen. Also the performances were used to identify the factors that encouraged engagement. Another reference [2] offers quantitative measures of engagement and identifies quite strongly how engagement can be fostered. [4] synthesizes existing research and frameworks on audience engagement with interactive digital art. The study [5] provides a Literature review about measuring emotional responses in interactive Installation art, including stimulation, multi-dimensional interactions and engagement. 


### References

[4] Edmonds, 2010. The art of interaction. Digital Creativity

[5] Chen, 2023. A Comprehensive Study of Emotional Responses in AI-Enhanced Interactive Installation Art
