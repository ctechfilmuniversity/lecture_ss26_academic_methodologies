### Learning from interactive museum installations about interaction design for public settings (Eva Hornecker, Matthias Stifter, 2006)

https://doi.org/10.1145/1228175.1228201

This paper evaluates a digitally-augmented museum exhibition using logfiles, observation, and interviews to identify what makes interactive installations engaging. The findings include that in the first seconds key factors in fostering appear to sustain visitor engagement across diverse audience groups.
