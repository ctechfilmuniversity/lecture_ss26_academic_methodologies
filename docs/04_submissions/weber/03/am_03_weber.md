### Task 03.02 - Storylines

Topic: Design Features and Engagement Threshold in Interactive Installations

I
1. I am working on a review of existing studies and data on engagement thresholds in curated exhibition spaces
2.  ... because I want to find out which design features, technical or spatial, affect the willingness of first-time visitors to engage with interactive installations
3. ...in order to identify how these features are associated with differences in visitor engagement threshold.



II.
1. The goal of this paper is to identify how technical or spatial design features affect the engagement threshold of first-time visitors in curated exhibition spaces.

2. How do design features of interactive installations in curated exhibition spaces affect the engagement threshold of first-time visitors?



III.

I answer the research question through a systematic literature review of existing studies on user engagement and interactive installations in curated exhibition spaces, identifying and categorizing recurring technical and spatial design features and describing how these are associated with differences in visitor engagement threshold.
